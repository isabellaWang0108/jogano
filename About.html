SHANGHAI JOGANO TRADING CO. LTD

  ——— Your Partners of creating wine’s value











Shanghai Jogano Trading Co. LTD. is a professional agent and marketing company importing premium wine. We specialize in introducing high quality wine with reasonable price from Europe to Chinese Market. With our pursuit of wine culture and quality, and insight of Chinese wine market, Jogano has many outstanding products from the old world now. We have been exclusive agent of more than 30 French wineries in most AOC regions, such as Chateau de Seguin, Chateau Cruzeau, Chateau Clauzet, JB Audy’s wine series of wine form right bank of Bordeaux, Andres Lurton Group and Burgandy’s products etc.

In 2005, Jogano was awarded the honor of China's best importer by Bordeaux Pomerol Wine Association.

Jogano not only guaranteed products’s quality with high value, but also provides wine education service for customers. We are against blind pursuit of some special brands. We believe the ability of understanding true value of different wines and enjoying different wine features, contributes to a better, healthier wine life
